OCS Afterparty - The Hyrule tour!

Goal:

Earn 6 points by playing songs for bosses.

Restrictions:

  • You must use the web app provided to know which song you can play for each boss.
  • No oldies! You may not play for bosses as adult songs that you learned as child.
  • Nocturne of Shadow and Song of Time are worth double each!
  • No Item manipulation. (No RBA)

    • Reminders:

  • Remember you’ll need to perform the ocarina items glitch to play songs inside boss rooms.
  • If you defeat a boss before playing the song for it on accident you can go back to the boss room and play it, still counts.
  • Songs played for the bosses only count after watching the intro cutscenes of the corresponding bosses
      or after they start attacking you in the case of mini-bosses.
  • You can still play songs as adult that you got as child for everything else except playing for the bosses in the table.
  • Time stops when hearing the “success” sound effect upon playing the last song for the last boss.
