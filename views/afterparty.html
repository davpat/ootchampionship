OoT Championship Series After Party - The Hyrule tour!

Goal:

Play 5 different songs for 5 different bosses.

Restrictions:

  • You may not play for bosses as adult songs that you learned as child.
  • No Item manipulation. (No RBA or GIM)
  • No using deku stick on B.

    • Reminders:

  • Remember you’ll need to perform the ocarina items glitch to play songs inside boss rooms.
  • After playing a song for a boss you may or may not defeat it depending on what you see fit.
  • Songs played for the bosses only count after watching the intro cutscenes of the corresponding bosses or after they start attacking you in the case of mini-bosses.
  • You can still play songs as adult that you got as child for everything else except playing for the bosses in the table.
  • Time stops when hearing the “success” sound effect upon playing the last song for the last boss.
