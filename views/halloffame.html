Impromptu Speedrunning

The Idea:

Basically, this is a real time race of OoT.

Everybody waits in chat for the signal, and at the "go", they all begin a new file.

The catch? The players learn the goal of the run only while waiting for the intro cutscene to finish. This means there are no pre-planned routes or practicing sections: you have to make up and execute your path on the fly.

Don't worry if you aren't the most knowledgeable in OoT â€” without premeditation, it is unlikely that obscure tricks will be thought of and used. Basically, all you need is decent skill, decent glitch knowledge, and the ability to think fast and play fast at the same time.

Since this is a new experience for everyone, it will probably be pretty chaotic, and anybody could win. Just post your progress every once in a while in irc during cutscenes so we can see the competition unfolding. Chances are, this will be great fun to everyone involved.

I'd imagine doing one impromptu race a month, on some given time such as the first saturday or last sunday. When the best time would be is up for discussion, as we want to incorporate as many people as possible.

Goal:

Fill up your whole equipment screen swords, shields, boots, etc.. You can decide yourself which items you want to get. In the end, there just has to be an item in every slot. You can decide between the quiver and the seed bag, for example.

*You must not RBA any items on your equipment screen, so you shouldn't try to RBA Cojiro, etc. Medallions, songs, etc., can be gotten through RBA, however *The run will end when Navi says your name at the cutscene before the Ganondorf Battle. Save Warping and Death Warping is allowed. *You can enter ganons castle anyway you see fit

If you are doing absolute or short recording instead of streaming, post where you are as much as possible, usually during cutscenes.
