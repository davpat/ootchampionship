OoT Championship Series - Information

OCS Season Race

Basically, this is a real time race of OoT.

Everybody waits in chat for the signal, and at the "go", they all begin a new file.

The catch? The players learn the goal of the run only while waiting for the intro cutscene to finish. This means there are no pre-planned routes or practicing sections: you have to make up and execute your path on the fly.

The OCS race will be hold every 2 weeks, on sunday, at 3:00 PM ET. When the best time would be is up for discussion, as we want to incorporate as many people as possible.

Finaly, the leaderboard will be hosted on this website and points will be distributed by race result.

OCS Playoff Race

After the 10 first races, the top 5 leaders in points will face each other in the playoff round.

For each playoff race, the player that finishes in last place will be eliminated and the process will repeat until only 2 players remain.

The top 2 players will face each other in the grand finals.
