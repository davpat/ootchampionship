Runnerguy vs Blinkzy - Round 1 - Match 2

We are almost over with the first round of the playoffs! It has been really entertaining so far, matchups were really exciting and every race had its own story, even categories!

It is now time for the second match between Runnerguy and Blinkzy!

But, which are their weakest points? Hard to tell, but i can point out the following.

Although RG knows almost every aspect of the game mechanics (including room shapes!) He barely has time to keep up with the new tricks and setups found, so he always attempts to use old school strats.

I didn’t really analyse Blinkzy, but he has for sure less knowledge of the game than RG, despite having the strats up to date (We will later see that this statement is false)

In conclusion, i think both players are really close to each other in terms of execution in general. RG has knowledge about stuff that nobody knows about (We need to remember that RG chose Spin the wheel in the first match, mostly because of that) But blinkzy is probably more confident about using new strats and tricks.

Let’s move on to this race in particular. We came here after RG winning the previous race, which was about getting all maps and compasses of the game, one of the possible goals that the wheel can deliver.

The last race was really close, both players finished with less than a minute of difference! It is now time for Blinkzy to choose the battle mode.

What will Blinkzy pick? Let’s analyse the options…

1. Bingo: If Blinkzy wants to secure the victory, he definitely needs to avoid Bingo. They are both very consistent with their times. However, blinkzy usually scores better than RG so it is not that bad choice after all.

2. Categories: We need to remember than Blinkzy banned categories during the first pick. Personally, i think Blinkzy is a bit favored with categories, RG has not ran any of the available ones in a while.

3. Draft: This is where game knowledge and decision making makes RG better than Blinkzy. RG has a strong experience on the drafting stage in both normal oot and beta quest. Knowing all the relations between goals is crucial for a good picking. I believe Blinkzy won’t go for this counter pick at all.

4. Short Lockout: Again, strategy is what short lockout is about. I don’t think any of them owns a great knowledge about child tricks and child bingo cards, this fact makes me believe that both players will attempt to avoid short lockout. Remember that RG banned this mode on the first match in the first place. Child bingos usually require contortion hovers, bomb hovers, precise superslides… Most of the crucial things can be made consistent using pause buffer, but neither of them play on VC.

5. Countdown: A trade off option for Blinkzy, RG hates countdowns, or at least, achieved a bad place in the OCS countdown race, which also was his first and last countdown up to date. Countdowns require a lot more of routing and time planning than execution, things that usually come with the experience. I personally believe Blinkzy is favored on this battle mode because, most likely, RG didn’t practise it at all.

It’s really hard to tell from my point of view what Blinkzy will pick, what does RG think about?



If i was Blinkzy, i would go for Countdown first, bingo second, and categories third.
