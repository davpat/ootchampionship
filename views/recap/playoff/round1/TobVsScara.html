The race starts! We reveal the card and we see the objectives. Remember that players are not allowed to go adult on a short lockout, if one of they claims a goal, the other player can’t claim it. The first to reach 13 points wins.

A lockout is like a chess game, every move you make matters. You need to be aware of what your opponent might be doing or going for.

The card has fast, normal and slow goals. Getting both skulltulas in lost woods is a fast one, while winning a bombchu bowling prize is a hard one, because it requires more items and time.
