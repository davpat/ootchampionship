Tob vs Scara - Round 1 - Match 2

The second match is about to begin! Scara is facing a great challenge. Tob still has a chance if he loses.

On the previous match, short lockout was played, resulting in a 13 - 9 in favour of Tob. It is now Scara’s turn to counter pick any race mode between the 5 remaining ones!

Will it be categories? We need to admit that Tob is pretty polyvalent on any mode, but we never saw Tob doing any kind of RTA, maybe he didn’t stream any of them? On the other hand, Scara did perform certain RTA categories in the past, like glitchless, no wrong warp, 37 water temple keys...

Will it be bingo or draft? This battle mode is, trust me, both player strongest points. I believe that Scara will avoid going for it since Tob usually scores better times on those than him.

Will it be countdown? Tob scored way better than Scara on the OCS countdown. Despite that, Tob banned countdown on his first banning pick. Is Tob afraid of it for some reason?

Finally, Spin the Wheel is the last option. If you want my opinion, although both players have a really deep knowledge of the game, i personally think Tob is better than Scara on this mode because (he plays on emulator) He is better on performing tricks in general.

If i was Scara, i would go, first for categories, second for spin the wheel and third for countdown, let’s see how it goes…
