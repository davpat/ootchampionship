Amateseru vs Juwk - W2 - Match 1

Here we go again! The winner’s second round is about to continue on this spectacular match between two stars!

The more we approach the end of the tournament, the less difference exists between matchups! Today we are going to witness 2 of the most brilliant oot players.

France is represented by Amateseru and Belgium by Juwk, two countries that are next to each other! We also expect a really close match. How could we describe them?

Amateseru, the polyvalent runner, has shown us that he has no weaknesses. A beast that quickly adapts to any situation of the game.

Juwk is like… Amazing as well. Probably as skilled as Ama, i would say. He has the ability to learn stuff really fast, trust me. The only reason juwk finished 8th during the regular season is because he needs, that extra step, a tiny little thing that could push him into the olympus, being more confident.

They are young, talented… Born for speedrunning, my friends. As i said, i’m willing to spectate this match, a new rivalry will be born.

Ama picks the game mode, as he always will (unless he loses the first final) What do i expect to happen? Sincerely, i’ve no idea, but i will try:

1. Ama will probably ban bingo if i have guess. Ama doesn’t lack of experience on bingo, but the problem is that Juwk is a veteran.

2. Juwk banning categories is a thing that could happen here, but, it won’t. Let’s study why. The player who picks categories as a game mode will lose the category pick. That just means that picking categories is perjudicial for Ama since Juwk would simply pick All Dungeons. Juwk will probably ban the wheel at this point.

3. Reached this far, Ama is going to ban short lockout or draft, he surely needs to eliminate any bingo related goal against Juwk.

4. Juwk will ban countdown or maybe categories if he doesn’t feel confident enough? Hard to tell, i would say banning countdown is the good move here

5. Ama has to choose between draft or categories, and he is going to pick draft to avoid playing no IM/WW or All Dungeons.

That’s my guess! If i read the players correctly, this race will either be categories or a bingo variant. I honestly prefer draft, because if categories get picked, this report will end right now.
