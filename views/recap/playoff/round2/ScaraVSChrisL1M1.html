Chris7 vs Scaramanga - Losers Round 1 - Match 1

It is 2018 and we keep going! Welcome everybody to the beginning of the losers bracket, a second chance for the players that lost a round on the main bracket.

Today we are about to witness, from my point of view, the real battle between N64 and VC. On my left, Chris, number #14 on the SRL boards. An experienced and polyvalent N64 runner that knows the game like the back of his hand. And, on my right, Scara, number #15 on the SRL boards, having the exact same description than Chris but playing on VC!

That’s why i believe this matchup is also between consoles; these two players are almost identical! I’m not only talking about their skill, they also share a common downside (I hope they forgive me for this) They seem to be clueless. They sometimes miss a routing option, an item, something that they could’ve done… It’s ok, unlike Tob, we are all humans. The good point about this is that, they tend to realize their mistakes later on, so that’s a way to keep improving!

Let’s move on to the banning phase. Yes, the first match of a round will always include a banning phase between modes, and i’m going to guess what’s going to happen:

1. Scara will almost for sure ban categories on his first pick, i can’t really see him banning any other thing here. Most of the OCS matchups banned pretty early this mode if one of the players was an active RTA runner.

2. I think Chris will ban short lockout here. I’ve never seem Chris practising child bingos. Besides that, N64 has a pretty disadvantage with VC with early goals that doesn’t require explosives. Chris already banned Short Lockout against Hyperion, and i believe he will do it again against Scara.

3. Scara has to choose between banning Bingo, Draft , Countdown or the Wheel at this point. I believe he will ban Bingo, but i’m not sure at all, mind games could start happening.

4. Chris will then either ban the wheel or draft. We need to remember that Chris banned draft against Hyperion during his first match. However, that probably was a well made decision because Hyperion’s strongest point is routing a huge set of objectives. I personally believe Chris will ban the Wheel here to remove the luck factor.

5. Between Draft or Countdown, i would say Scara can pick either one, can’t really see a huge difference between these two.

All said, now we are going to wait for the players to make the real banning phase. You will see if i did my homework, correctly analysing players, or if i’m totally wrong. The banning stage could go in different ways but this match is going to be a mode between Bingo, Draft or Countdown. It is now time to wait for the sunset to come in Europe…
